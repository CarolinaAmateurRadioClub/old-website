Welcome to the Carolina Amateur Radio Club Site

It is the intent of this club to establish an Amateur Radio communication system which will enhance the capability of radio amateurs to provide communications in the public interest, further the technical capabilities of the members, and interest and entice non amateurs into the ranks of amateur radio operators.
